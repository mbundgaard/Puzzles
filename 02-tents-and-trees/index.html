Tents and Trees

Puzzle #02

Place tents next to trees

How to Play

  • Left-click to place or remove a tent
  • Right-click to mark a cell as empty (×)
  • Each tent must be next to exactly one tree (up/down/left/right)
  • Each tree pairs with exactly one tent
  • Tents cannot touch each other, even diagonally
  • Numbers show how many tents belong in each row/column
